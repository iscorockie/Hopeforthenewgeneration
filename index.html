Our Story

From the Rugby Field to Community Transformation

Founded by Uganda Rugby Cranes captain Ivan Magomu, Hope for the Next Generation was born from a belief that sports can be a powerful vehicle for social change. What started as informal coaching sessions in Mbale has grown into a registered NGO reaching thousands of vulnerable children across Eastern Uganda.
