WHAT WE DO

Four pillars of lasting change

We don't just help — we equip the next generation with tools, skills, and confidence to rise above challenges.
📚
Education & Life Skills

School fees support, tutoring, and life-skills training that break the cycle of poverty.

  • 240 students supported
  • Vocational training
🏉
Grassroots Sports

Rugby and community sports that teach discipline, teamwork, and leadership.

  • 18 youth teams
  • Annual tournaments
⚖️
Child Rights & Justice

Advocacy, counseling, and rehabilitation for children in conflict with the law.

  • Legal aid support
  • Family reintegration
👩
Gender Equality

Girls' empowerment camps, women's livelihood training, and inclusion for PWDs.

  • 12 girls camps held
  • Skills for income
