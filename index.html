Who We Are

A catalyst for lasting change in Eastern Uganda.

Hope for the Next Generation (HNG) is a registered community-based organisation working at the intersection of child protection, gender equality, and education in Mbale City.

We believe that true transformation happens at the grassroots. By amplifying the voices of our beneficiaries and aligning with global sustainable development frameworks, we are building a safer, more equitable future for the next generation.

Our Core Programs

Targeted interventions designed to uplift, educate, and protect.

Grassroots Sports & Play

Using sports as a vehicle for life skills training, community cohesion, and physical well-being among youth.

Quality Education

Ensuring access to inclusive, equitable education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities for all children.

Child Protection & Advocacy

Creating safe environments and advocating for the rights of girls and vulnerable youth in our communities.
