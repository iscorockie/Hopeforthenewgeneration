About Hope for the Next Generation

Our Story

Founded by Ivan Magomu, Uganda Rugby Cranes Captain and Sports Lawyer, HNG uses the transformative power of sport and education to restore hope to vulnerable children and youth in Mbale City.

We believe every child deserves a chance to dream, learn, and become a leader.

Our Programmes

1. Education & Life Skills

The Challenge

High school dropout rates, lack of learning materials, and limited access to quality education in Mbale communities.

Key Results:
• 850+ children supported with school materials
• After-school tutoring programmes
• Life skills workshops

Our Approach

We provide school fees support, scholastic materials, mentorship, and life skills training. We work directly with schools and families to ensure children stay in education and develop holistically.

2. Grassroots Sports & Talent Development

The Challenge

Limited access to sports facilities and structured activities, leading to missed opportunities for discipline, teamwork, and talent discovery.

Our Approach

Using rugby and other sports as vehicles for personal development. We run regular training sessions, tournaments, and life-skills coaching on the field.

Key Results:
• Multiple youth rugby teams
• Talent identification pathway
• Leadership development through sport

3. Child Protection & Juvenile Justice

Our Approach

Prevention of abuse, case management, counselling, and collaboration with local authorities to ensure children’s rights are protected and access to justice is improved.

Impact

Children reintegrated into families and schools, safer communities, and stronger child protection systems.

4. Girls Empowerment & Gender Equality

The Challenge

Gender-based violence, early marriage, limited opportunities for girls, and exclusion of persons with disabilities.

Our Approach

Girls’ camps, leadership training, livelihood skills, mentorship, and mainstreaming disability inclusion across all programmes.

5. Community Sustainability & Livelihoods

Our Approach

Vocational training, sustainable agriculture, entrepreneurship support, and environmental awareness to build resilient families and communities.

Key Results

Improved household incomes, climate-resilient practices, and stronger community ownership of development.
