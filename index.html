What We Do

Four Tracks of Real Impact

Our integrated, community-led approach builds resilience through four interconnected programme areas — each addressing a critical dimension of vulnerability in Mbale.

Support a Programme
Track 01
📚
Education &
Life-Skills
School-support & quality learning

We deliver school bursaries, learning materials, mentorship, and life-skills training to vulnerable children and young persons — systematically breaking intergenerational cycles of poverty and building confident, capable young minds.

School Bursaries Literacy Camps Mentorship Life-Skills Training

Rugby, wellness & holistic development

Using rugby and community sports as powerful tools for discipline, teamwork, and physical wellness — we channel youth energy into structured programmes that develop confident athletes, resilient leaders, and future change-makers in Mbale.

Rugby Academies Talent Scouting Coaching Clinics Sports Equipment

Track 03
⚖️
Advocacy &
Juvenile Justice
Child rights, protection & rehabilitation

We advocate for juvenile justice and child rights protection — actively preventing abuse and violence while providing professional rehabilitation, counselling, and restorative support services to affected children, youth, and their caregivers across Mbale.

Legal Aid Counselling Safe Spaces Policy Advocacy

Girls, women & PWDs

Through targeted outreach, girls' camps, skills-building workshops, and livelihood training, we empower girls, women, and Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) — promoting genuine gender equality, inclusion, and sustainable self-reliance across communities.

Accountability & Transparency

We Earn Your Trust
Every Day

Community sustainability and family improvement sit at the heart of all we do. We are committed to radical transparency so donors worldwide can give with full confidence.

🌿
Tackling Poverty & Health

We partner with families to deliver training, health education, and access to life-changing solutions that address poverty, health, and environmental challenges at the household level.

🤝
Community-Led Innovation

Our collaborative projects are designed and driven by local people — ensuring every intervention is culturally grounded, sustainable, and genuinely improves livelihoods for disadvantaged families.

📊
Financial Accountability

We publish annual reports, audited accounts, and programme outcome data. Our independent board reviews all financial decisions with zero tolerance for misuse of donor funds.

🌍
Environmental Responsibility

From tree-planting drives to sustainable farming demonstrations, our programmes integrate environmental stewardship as a core pillar of long-term community resilience.
